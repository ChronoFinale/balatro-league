# About the League

## What it is

The Balatro League is a recurring, **skill-tiered** league for Balatro
Multiplayer. Each season you're placed with players around your own level, play a
round-robin against your group, and **move up or down** between seasons based on how
you finish. It runs in Discord (matches, ban/pick, results) with a companion website
for standings, profiles, and history.

## Why it exists

Pick-up BMP games are great, but they're a coin flip — you either stomp someone or
get stomped, and there's no thread connecting one game to the next. The league fixes
both:

- **Fair games.** Tiers and divisions mean you're matched against people your own
  size. A new player gets real, winnable games; a strong player gets real
  competition. Nobody's wasting their time.
- **Something to play *for*.** Every match counts toward your division standings,
  and finishing well **promotes** you up a tier next season. There's a ladder to
  climb and a record that follows you.
- **A reason to get better.** Because you play people just above and below you, the
  gap is always close enough to learn from — and the players above you are there to
  **coach**, not just to win.

## What it's trying to do

1. **Give everyone competitive games at their level** — from first-season rookies to
   the top tier.
2. **Reward improvement** — climb the tiers, build a season-over-season record, earn
   your rank.
3. **Build a community around BMP** — teammates, rivals, coaching, banter, and a
   shared season everyone's playing through at the same time.
4. **Keep it friendly.** It's competitive, but at the end of the day it's a game we
   all play because it's fun. Good sportsmanship is the baseline.

## Who it's for

Anyone who plays Balatro Multiplayer and wants more than random matchmaking —
whether you're here to grind to the top tier, to get coaching and improve, or just
to have a regular set of games against people you'll see again. No skill floor: you
start where you start, and the league sorts the rest out.

## The vibe

Competitive **and** welcoming. Play hard, be a good opponent, help the people coming
up behind you, and enjoy the run. That's the whole thing.
